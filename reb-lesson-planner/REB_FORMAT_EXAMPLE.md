## SPECIAL NEEDS / INCLUSIVE EDUCATION NOTES

**For Visual Impairment:** Provide enlarged fraction diagrams (at least 3x normal size), texture-based fractions (raised lines), and verbal descriptions of all visual examples.

**For Hearing Impairment:** Use visual demonstrations extensively, write key terms on board, provide written instructions, pair with a supportive peer.

**For Physical Disabilities:** Ensure manipulatives can be handled independently or provide adapted gripping tools. Arrange seating for easy participation.

**For Learning Disabilities:** Pre-teach vocabulary, break activities into smaller steps, provide visual supports, use concrete materials before abstract representation.

**For Gifted Learners:** Challenge with improper fractions, equivalent fractions, and word problems involving multiple steps.
