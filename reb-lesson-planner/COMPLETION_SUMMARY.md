## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Best Results:

1. **Get REB Syllabus First**
   - Download from https://elearning.reb.rw/
   - PDF has key competencies for each unit
   - Reference while generating lessons

2. **Paste Key Competence Exactly**
   - Copy Word-for-Word from REB syllabus
   - Ensures perfect alignment
   - AI knows exactly what you're teaching

3. **Specify Learning Objective Clearly**
   - Use action verbs (Create, Analyze, Evaluate)
   - Make it specific and measurable
   - Better objective = better lesson

4. **Review Before Teaching**
   - Always check generated lesson
   - Verify facts and examples
   - Customize with your class context
   - Add local examples/stories

5. **Customize for Your Students**
   - AI generates generic lesson
   - You personalize it
   - Your local context matters!
   - Makes lessons more engaging

6. **Export & Backup**
   - Download PDF regularly
   - Keep copies on computer
   - Never lose your lessons
   - Easy to share with colleagues
