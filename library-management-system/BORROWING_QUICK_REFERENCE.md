## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long can I keep a book?
**A:** 14 days from the day it's approved and checked out

### Q: What if I can't finish by the due date?
**A:** You can renew the book (extend by 14 more days) - ask librarian

### Q: What happens if I return books late?
**A:** Late fee of ‚Ç±0.50 per day per book is charged

### Q: Can I request a book that's already borrowed?
**A:** Yes! If multiple copies exist, you'll request one. If it's the only copy, your request will be denied

### Q: How do I check the book I requested?
**A:** Check with the librarian in person or ask when you plan to collect

### Q: Can I request books through a friend?
**A:** No - each person must request their own books using their account

### Q: What if I lose a borrowed book?
**A:** Report it to the librarian immediately. You may be charged the book's replacement cost

### Q: Can I request books from home?
**A:** Yes! This system works from anywhere - just login online

### Q: How many books can I borrow?
**A:** Maximum 5 books at a time. Return some to request more

### Q: What if I never get a response to my request?
**A:** Follow up with the librarian after 2 days
