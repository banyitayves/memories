## How to Request a Book for Borrowing

### Step 1: Find the Book

1. Go to **"Search OPAC"** from the sidebar
2. Browse books or search for a specific title
3. Look for books with **‚úì Available** status (green)

### Step 2: Click "Request to Borrow"

1. Find the book you want
2. Click the **"üì§ Request to Borrow"** button

### Step 3: Fill the Request Form

The form will show:

| Field | Description | Required? |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| Book Title | Shows the book you selected | Auto-filled |
| Your Email | Your school email | Auto-filled |
| Your Role | Your role (Student/Staff) | Auto-filled |
| Reason | Why you want to borrow this book | Optional |

**Example Reason:**
- "Needed for literature class project"
- "Personal reading interest"
- "Research assignment"

### Step 4: Submit Your Request

1. Click **"Submit Request"** button
2. See confirmation message: "Your borrow request has been submitted!"
3. The librarian will review your request

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long can I keep a borrowed book?
**A:** Standard checkout period is **14 days**. You'll see the due date when your request is approved.

### Q: Can I renew a book?
**A:** Yes! You can renew your books if no one else has requested them. Contact the librarian or use the system if the renewal feature is available.

### Q: What if I return a book late?
**A:** Late fees are **‚Ç±0.50 per day** per item. It's best to return books on time.

### Q: How many books can I borrow at once?
**A:** You can borrow up to **5 items** at the same time.

### Q: What if the book isn't available?
**A:** If a book shows "‚úó Not Available", it means all copies are currently borrowed. You can request it anyway, and you'll be notified when it becomes available.

### Q: Can I request multiple books?
**A:** Yes! You can submit requests for multiple books. Submit each request separately.

### Q: What if my request is denied?
**A:** The librarian will provide a reason. Common reasons include:
- Book is reserved for another user
- Exceeding maximum items limit
- Overdue fines pending
